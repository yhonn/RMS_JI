TITLE

Building a skills of 500 youth passion fruit farmers in passion fruit growing, marketing and enhancing access to finance through partnership with private sector actors in Mubende between December 2016 to June 2017

PURPOSE

Working with private sector players in Mubende including, K-Mubende and Kikomeko Allan and other technical persons in grafting technology, the youth interested in this enterprise will be trained on pest and disease management, business planning, market linkages and given sustainable access to agro inputs and financial services. YLA intends to increase the access of the passion fruit farmers to extension services, market information and building their skills in grafting of passion fruit and management of passion fruit nurseries.
